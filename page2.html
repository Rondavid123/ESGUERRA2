What Is a Floor Plan?

A floor plan is a sort of drawing that depicts a home or other property's layout from above. Typically, floor plans show where walls, windows, doors, and stairs are located in addition to fixed installations like kitchen cabinets, bathroom fixtures, and appliances. Room kinds, room sizes, and wall lengths are typically shown on floor plans, which are typically drawn to scale. They might also display furniture arrangements and incorporate outdoor spaces.
